Non invasive

Easy to use

Easily attach and detach – Insert the lock quickly through the small hinge gap between the screen and keyboard.
Compatibility
  • 13” MacBook Pro with Retina display
  • 15” MacBook Pro with Retina display
  • Supports any standard Kensington lock
Working on a flat surface
While working on a flat surface, such as a table or desk, the lock does not cause the MacBook Pro to rise. This allows you on getting your work done without ever noticing the lock is in place.
Using a riser
Using a laptop riser does not restrict the lock usage in any way.
Smooth finish
The lock is 3D printed with great precision without any sharp edges. This way damage to your MacBook Pro is minimized while placing and removing the lock.
